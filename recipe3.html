Raspberry-Orange Sparklers

Level:Easy     Prep:5min     Yield: 1 drink
Total:15min    


****choose and put into the shopping list

INGREDIENTS:

 1 cup raspberries
1/2 cup mint
1/2 cup sugar
raspberries
1/2 ounce of puree
1/2 ounce of orange-flavored vodka

DIRECTIONS:

Pulse 1 cup raspberries and 1/2 cup each mint and sugar in a food processor.
Let sit 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, then strain through a fine mesh sieve.
each drink, combine 1/2 ounce each of the puree and orange-flavored vodka in a glass;
top with sparkling white wine and garnish with raspberries.
